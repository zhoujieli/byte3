Quality: About This Data Set

Quality of the dataset is really out of my control since it heavily depends on the reliability of AWARE framework and the phone's operating system, for example, whether the phone blocked the AWARE app from collecting data.

Is the data complete?

The data is complete enough to show some patterns in the way I move between School, Sterling Plaza and my apartments. For the battery temperature and battery level, the frequency is very nice so I would say that the data is complete enough. One concern is that since my phone might block AWARE from time to time, so some data would be missing. However, based on the plots, it is not a big issue.

Is the data coherent?

The data is coherent in that the level of battery charge, the temperature, the air pressure and my locations adhere to my expectations. The distributions and the range of the values are also sensible, for example temperature around 32 celcius degree normally, and 40 degrees when in heavy use. Air pressure is also accurate.

Is the data correct?

I can verify the air pressure, so I googled for the air pressure from Feb 19 to March 6 in Pittsburgh and the stats mostly aligned.

Is the data Accountable?

I trust CMU's AWARE framework and Huawei Mate 9, and nothing controversial is present in the data visualization, so yes, data is accountable.
