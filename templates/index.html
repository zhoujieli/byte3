Visual Narrative of a Mobile Data Set

Data generated by Jieli Zhou's Huawei Mate 9 from Feb 19, 2018 to March 6, 2018 in Pittsburgh.

Clear trends in battery level of my phone can be detected and the rate of usage is also clear from this plot. This can also be used to test whether Huawei lied about their battery usage time. From this plot, the usage time is good, around 12 hours per charge and I used my phone moderately.

The plot of battery temperature can reveal the times when I was heavily using the phone, say gaming. This corresponds to my gaming habits pretty well, for example in the afternoon when I was bored or after midnight when I was done with my work.
